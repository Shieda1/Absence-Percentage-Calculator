What is the absence percentage calculator?
The absence percentage calculator is a tool that measures the absenteeism rate of the employees inside a company.
How to calculate absence percentage?
To calculate absence percentage you should use the following formula:
absenteeism rate = (total days absent / (total employees * total workdays)) * 100
Why use absence percentage calculator?
The absence percentage calculator is used in order to calculate the absenteeism rate of the employees inside a company.
